My Mindset & Point of View

People know me for very different things. Some believe I'm a programming language designer, some remember me from the GNU project, some use the tools I developed for some open source projects, few know me by the questions I ask, and the questions that I answer, one person thinks I'm a typographer†1. For myself, I have no idea...

1
He also thinks I'm a mexican which in the days that I eat Tacco when wearing Poncho, I really wanna be.

What I can say is ever since I was a kiddo, I wanted to be an Inventor. I liked creating really complex systems, fine arts, music, design, algebra, etc. And being an inventor was the only job title where I could combine these things together; the only job where I could be really creative. I did my best to become an inventor and these days, I don't know, I'm doing many things and creating many stuff, which is what I wanted. I consult companies, I create tools, compose music, design things, make products, create brands, all sorts of things that I liked.
